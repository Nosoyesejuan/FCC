Who is Harold Trompetero?


Creative, basically that is what he is, a person who likes to create things, who likes nature a lot. He travels a lot, he likes to walk a lot, planting plants. His life is his wife, nature and creativity, almost all 3 at the same level. That's him, a creative who lives life.


He has been the director of series and soap operas for television such as Cartas a Harrison (1996), Tamayo familiar (1997) and Amor a mil (2001), as well as more than a hundred commercials, music videos and video art productions. His first feature film was Diástole y sístole, los movimientos del corazón, awarded in the categories of Best Actor, Best Actress and the Audience Award at the Cartagena de Indias 2000 International Film Festival and the Audience Award at the Si Si Festival in Toronto.
